---
title: "Italian Sausage Gnocchi"
time_active: 2
time_total: 10
servings: 2
cuisine: ["Italian"]
category: ["Main", "Lunch"]
diet: ["Meat"]
occasion: ["Weeknight"]
ingredients:
  - "500 | Gnocchi (g)"
  - "6 | Italian sausages"
  - "200 | Frozen peas (g)"
  - "50 | Parmesan cheese (g)"
  - "2 | Garlic cloves (sliced)"
  - "0.5 | Chili flakes (tsp)"
  - "to taste | Black pepper"
  - "Olive oil"
notes:
  - "By Rune"
---

Slice the garlic into thin slices (1mm thick).

Bring a pan of water to a boil for the gnocchi.

Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat with olive oil.

Cut open sausages, crumble the meat into the pan in bite-sized chunks.

Brown the sausage chunks on both sides, then lower the heat.

Add garlic slices and chili flakes; sweat for 1-2 minutes.

Salt the boiling water and cook gnocchi (3-4 minutes).

Add frozen peas to the frying pan and increase heat to medium-high.

Drain gnocchi, keeping 1 glass of cooking water.

Add gnocchi, cooking water, and 2/3 of the Parmesan to the frying pan.

Stir until the water, cheese, and fat form a glistening coat.

Serve garnished with remaining Parmesan and black pepper.
