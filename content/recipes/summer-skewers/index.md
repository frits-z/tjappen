---
title: "Summer Skewers"
description: "A refreshing caprese-style summer appetizer with an optional Italian ham twist, perfect for a beach day or road trip."
time_active: 15
time_total: 15
servings: 20
cuisine: ["Italian"]
category: ["Salad"]
diet: ["Vegetarian", "Meat"]
occasion: ["Weeknight", "Special"]
ingredients:
  - "40 | Cherry tomatoes (~350-400gr)"
  - "20 | Mini mozzarella balls (~250 gr)"
  - "20 | Leaves of basil"
  - "4 | Classic Cremaceto 55% balsamic vinegar (tsp)"
  - "2 | Olive oil (tsp)"
  - "1 | Honey (tsp)"
  - "Salt to taste"
  - "Freshly ground black pepper to taste"
  - "20 | Small slices of Italian ham (Optional, e.g., half a slice of parma ham per skewer)"
notes:
  - "Top tip: Great refreshing food for a day on the beach, or on a road trip."
---

In a small mixing bowl, thoroughly mix the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and honey.

Add Salt and pepper to your preferences (5-6 turns of pepper and 2 turns of salt is recommended).

Drain your mozzarella balls and wash your cherry tomatoes.

Add the mozzarella and cherry tomatoes to the mixing bowl, and coat them in the sauce.

For the skewers, layer them as follows: Cherry Tomato, basil, optional ham slice, mini mozzarella ball, and finish with another Cherry tomato. Enjoy!
