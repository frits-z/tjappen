---
title: "Jackie's Spinach Casserole"
time_active: 10
time_total: 40
servings: 4
cuisine: ["Dutch"]
category: ["Main"]
diet: ["Meat"]
occasion: ["Weeknight"]
ingredients:
  - "# Main"
  - "500 | Frozen spinach (g)"
  - "250 | (Vegan) minced meat (g)"
  - "1 | Bag grated mild cheese"
  - "4 | Eggs"
  - "4 | Slices of thawed puff pastry"
  - "# On the side"
  - "600 | Potato slices (g)"
notes:
  - "By Jackie"
---

Preheat the oven to 200C (conventional oven).

Thaw the frozen spinach in the microwave.

Cook the (vegan) minced meat in a pan until brown and crumbly. Meanwhile, boil the 4 eggs for about 10 minutes until hard-boiled.

In an oven dish, mix the thawed spinach, the cooked minced meat, and the grated cheese. Peel and slice the boiled eggs, and add them to the dish. Cover the top of the mixture with the slices of puff pastry.

Bake the spinach casserole in the oven for about 30 minutes. 

Meanwhile, bake or fry the potato slices until golden and crispy. Serve together. Enjoy your meal!
