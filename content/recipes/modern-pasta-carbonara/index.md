---
title: "Modern Pasta Carbonara"
time_active: 5
time_total: 30
servings: 2
cuisine: ["Italian"]
course: ["Main"]
diet: ["Meat"]
effort: ["Advanced"]
ingredients:
  - "250 | Pasta (g)"
  - "200 | Guanciale (g)"
  - "7 | Green asparagus"
  - "4 | Eggs (1 whole + 3 yolks)"
  - "150 | Parmesan cheese (g)"
  - "to taste | Freshly ground Black pepper"
notes:
  - "By Rune"
---

Dice the asparagus and guanciale. Mix the eggs with grated parmesan into a semi-liquid paste.

Boil salted water and cook the pasta al dente.

Fry the guanciale in a dry pan until the fat renders out, then add the asparagus and lots of pepper. Fry until the asparagus is cooked and guanciale is crispy.

Drain the pasta, but keep 1 glass of pasta water aside.

Mix the pasta with the guanciale and asparagus in the pan (off the heat or very low heat).

Slowly whisk a few tablespoons of pasta water into the egg-parmesan mixture to temper it.

Off the heat entirely, pour the egg mixture into the pasta and toss vigorously until it becomes a creamy and thick sauce. Add more pasta water if it's too thick.

Serve immediately with extra cheese and freshly ground pepper.
