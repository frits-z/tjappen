---
title: "Sweet potato salad"
time_active: 20
time_total: 40
servings: 2
cuisine: ["European"]
category: ["Main", "Salad"]
diet: ["Vegetarian"]
occasion: ["Weeknight"]
ingredients:
  - "500 | Sweet potatoes (g)"
  - "1 | Large carrot"
  - "100 | Asparagus tips (g)"
  - "1 | Red onion"
  - "0.5 | Lemon"
  - "100 | Feta (g)"
  - "85 | Lamb's lettuce (g)"
  - "Olive oil"
  - "2 | Handfuls of walnuts"
notes:
  - "By Rune"
---

Preheat the oven to 200C (conventional oven).

Dice sweet potato and carrot into half-circles (approx. 0.5cm thick) and place on a baking tray.

Add olive oil, salt, and pepper; bake for 20-25 minutes.

Add asparagus tips and bake for another 10 minutes.

Finely dice the red onion, extract lemon juice, and crumble the feta.

In a bowl, mix the roasted vegetables, lamb's lettuce, onion, and feta.

Whisk lemon juice with 2 tablespoons of olive oil.

Add dressing to the salad and mix thoroughly.

Garnish with walnuts.
